Eat fruits be Healty

Fruitopia is a fruit-flavored drink brand introduced by Coca-Cola in 1994, designed to compete in the adult juice market with popular, distinctively named flavors like Strawberry Passion Awareness. Despite initial popularity and a massive marketing campaign, it was discontinued in the US by 2003, though it remains available in Canada and Australia.
